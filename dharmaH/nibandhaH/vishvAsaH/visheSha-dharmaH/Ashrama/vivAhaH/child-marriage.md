- Child marriage is referred to in the distant past, but very rarely.
- Note: It should not be considered inferior to adult marriage out of hand.

## prauDha-vivAha evidence
Consummation ritual ought to happen on the fourth day of marriage - this indicates that adult marriage was intended. But later practice changed this and introduced a delay.

Apastamba - Soon after marriage, when yoktra is removed, Ap. says take her home. "योक्त्रं विमुच्य, तां ततः प्र वा वाहयेत् प्र वा हारयेत्". Upon bringing her home, requires brahmacharya for 3 nights. "त्रिरात्रमुभयोरधश् शय्या ब्रह्मचर्यं क्षारलवणवर्जनं च". So, that makese sense only when the girl is prauDhA.


- Common ages of marriage, classically, were: 20 for men (having completed 12 years of education starting at 8) and about 16 for women.
