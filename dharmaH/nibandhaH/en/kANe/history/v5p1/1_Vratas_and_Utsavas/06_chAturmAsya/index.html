The Y─Бj├▒avalkya-smr╠еti 4 (I. 125) makes it obligatory on a person of means to offer a Soma sacrifice every year, a pa┼Ыubandha in each ayana, the ─Аgrayaс╣Зeс╣гс╣нi (performed when new crops are ready) and the c─Бturm─Бsyas. The Y─Бj├▒avalkya-smr╠еti employs the word vrata in the sense of pr─Бya┼Ыcitta in III. 251, 252, 254, 266, 269, 282, 298, 300, in the sense of ‘brahmacarya’ in III, 15, and in the sense of the food to be subsisted upon in III, 289, but hardly ever in the sense of a rite to be observed on a tithi or week day or nakс╣гatra as in the pur─Бс╣Зas.
