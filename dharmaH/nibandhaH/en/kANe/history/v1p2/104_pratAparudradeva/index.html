The Sarasvativil─Бsa was compiled by Prat─Бparudradeva, a king of the Gajapati dynasty who ruled at Cuttack (Katakanagari) in Orissa. The Dayabh─Бga portion of this work was published with an English translation by Rev. Thomas Foulkes in 1881.
